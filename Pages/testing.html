Project Description

For our System Administration project, we chose to run a Multiplayer server for the game Garry's Mod. Garry's Mod is a sandbox (free to do whatever) game, which doubles as a host for a variety of other games; such as Prop Hunt and TTT.

We chose to create a server for TTT; Trouble in Terrorist Town.

Above is what every player sees when loading into our server; our take on a Welcome Screen.

Once the game starts, all of the players are sorted between 3 roles: Detective, Terrorist, and Innocent. There is usually only one Detective, who's purpose is to weed out the suspicious players (Terrorists) that are trying to kill everyone else. The Terrorist's job is to eliminate the remaining players while also staying alive, with the Innocents trying their best to survive and determine who is guilty.
