My website, formally known as Poodle Scheduler, was created for the midterm of my CSI 3150 - Web and Mobile Systems class. In this class, we were tasked with creating a website using the generic combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This was my first attempt at venturing into the world of web design!

My inspiration directly came from Google Calendar. I have had a Google phone for over 6 years (Google Pixel 2 XL, then recently the Google Pixel 6), and have loved the feature of setting reminders and having those sync to the Google Calendar. I have used this feature everyday, and it has helped tremendously

Thus, I strove to create a similar product. With Poodle Scheduler, I wanted to bring back the Agendas that I used to use for Elementary and Middle School. In this sense, I allowed the user to create and delete reminders during the scope of the project; which happened to be until the end of 2022. *More info*

To fit the name, I embarked on creating a Puppy Slideshow to showcase my other passion; photography and animals. I included simplistic and funny pictures of my dog, Mia, along with my friend and Grandparent's dog. In the midst, I also added a few photos I took myself of nature. This lightened the seriousness of the project, and was a lot of fun to make.
