Project Description

As a background, I have had two Google Pixel phones (2XL and 6), and love the Google Calendar features of setting and viewing reminders. Thus came the idea of creating my own spin on a reminder-setting and calendar-based website application.

To add my own twist, I incorporated my beloved childhood dog, Mia, into my website. I created a 'Puppy Slideshow' to showcase my dog, my friend's dog, and a few landscape pictures I've taken. Additionally, I named the website after her, plus the webicon.

With this project starting in the fall, I limited the scope of the app to the remaining months of the year; October, November, and December. Within each month, you can navigate between months, navigate to other non-month pages, create a reminder, or delete reminders.
