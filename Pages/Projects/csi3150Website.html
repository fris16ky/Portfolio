Poodle Scheduler

The scope of this project was to create a functioning, accessible website for any topic of our choosing. Personally, I have a deep affinity for both dogs and the Google system; I have had

a Google Pixel (2XL and 6) for the last 5 years, and have used the "Set a Reminder" feature every day. Instead of creating a mimic or knock-off, I chose to go with nostalgia; Agendas.

Back in Elementary and Middle School I would write down my day-to-day details in a personal Agenda. Thus sprung the idea to modernize it into a website, with some playful additions.

The project was more challenging than initially expected, so I conceded to simplify my website to only contain three months; the duration of my Project while in class.
