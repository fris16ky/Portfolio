Project Requirements

Our general task was to find a group and to separate into three roles; Product Owner, Scrum Master, and the Development Team. I was apart of the Development Team along with two others, meaning we were tasked with developing our application. Aside from that, we were allowed to explore any options for our app, but we had to keep track of our progress, which we did via GitHub and Monday.


Project Description

I was directly involved with choosing our project idea, where I took inspiration from Type to Learn, which I was introduced to during Elementary and Middle School.

Akin to Type to Learn, we wanted to improve our user's ability to type efficiently, although the former was for computer keyboards. Thus, we shifted our vision to the crucial skill that is typing on your phone.
