Project Requirements

For our final project, my partner and I were in charge of creating a Project Report consisting of an abstract, business rules, data directory, entity relationship model, relational database model, and some queries with our data.


Project Description

Being familiar with the subject, we chose to revolve our database around the school system, specifically university. We kept track of a variety of information, from Head of Department to Tutors and Students. In total we created 10 entities with 27 unique attributes.


After designing the database, we populated each table with 3-5 data points, with five queries to return different information from our database.
